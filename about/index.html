About me

I am Federico Ramacciotti, Computer Science student at the University of Pisa and programmer.

I’ve made two little projects by myself, Water Reminder and Simple Tasks, written in Go, a language that I learned by myself. Moreover, I made other projects for my Univerity, written in C, Java and a smaller one in OCaml. I’m still quite unexperienced but always ready to work and learn new things. In University I also learned a little bit of SQL.

My interests are imperative and object-oriented languages, in fact I want to learn much more about Go and Python, Unix and Operating Systems in general and the backend side of the applications. Aside from coding related interests, I really like traveling and doing sports, learning new things and playing video games.

I would like to be contacted by you if you’re interested. In case, you can see my code at github.com/0xfederama reach me at vgfede@gmail.com.
